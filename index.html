Hi, I’m a PhD student in Cap-lab (PI: Gilles Pourtois; https://www.cap-lab.be/) at Ghent University, Belgium.

I am generally interested in neurocognitive mechanisms of favorable influences of negative affect (punishment, negative feedback) on cognitive control, and how does it change with further regulation of negative affect by means of cognitive reappraisal.

During my master period, my research topic mainly focused on emotional conflict. I investigated how the processing priority of negative emotion improved emotional conflict control, as well as how was emotional conflict modulated by perceptual load.
